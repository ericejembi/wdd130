Makurdi

In the heart of Benue State, Makurdi, a city nestled along the Benue River, pulsed with a unique rhythm, a blend of old traditions and modern aspirations, where the echoes of history mingled with the sounds of daily life. The Benue River, a lifeline for the city, shaped its identity, with its banks serving as a meeting place for locals and a source of sustenance. Makurdi's population, a mix of Tiv people, Idoma people and other ethnic groups, contributed to a rich cultural landscape, evident in the vibrant markets, traditional dances, and local cuisines. Makurdi remained a place of hope, where dreams were nurtured, and where people came together to build a brighter tomorrow.
